Frequently Asked Questions

What Is The Default Password?

The default password is raspberry

Will This Run My Pi 3B+ ?

While Twister OS will install on the 3B+ it is not recomended. Twister OS was designed and build around the Pi4 and you may experience issues installing on the 3B+.

How Do I Change The Password

Open Terminal and type passwd - follow the prompts to change your password.

Is Twister OS 64bit?

Twister OS is a 32bit Operating System.
